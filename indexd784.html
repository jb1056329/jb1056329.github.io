Kawa http://www.getkawa.com Your Coffee App in Hong Kong Sun, 19 Mar 2017 12:56:13 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.7.3 http://www.getkawa.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/cropped-Kawa-icon-32x32.jpg Kawa http://www.getkawa.com 32 32 La Station http://www.getkawa.com/?p=2241 Thu, 10 Dec 2015 06:45:52 +0000 http://ec2-54-169-116-191.ap-southeast-1.compute.amazonaws.com/?p=2241 Just a few steps away from Southorn Playground where the players never seem to stop shooting balls, begins the Wan Chai Market. An area filled with all kinds of goods and commodities, tucked under colorful umbrellas with people navigating their way through the crowd. No matter if you are looking for a new scarf, a toy for your nephews birthday or just some fruits, you will find it here. But as we all know (men maybe more than women) shopping can be quite tiring. To get away from the buzz of the market, you might want to consider heading to the station – not the Wan Chai MTR station but La Station.

La Station is a small coffeeshop decorated with white and red tiles and gorgeous golden lanterns, smelling of freshly ground coffee and pastry, making you feel like you are in the best train waiting area ever.

There are fresh flowers on each table and the big windows offer a great spot for observing people while sipping your cappuccino. The menu features exotic coffee variations, like rose latte or a cappuccino brulée for those who want to try something different. The pastries and cheesy paninis are delicious and for every yogurt fan I highly recommend the yogurt with cake crumble.

]]> SFS Coffee http://www.getkawa.com/?p=2229 Thu, 10 Dec 2015 04:54:10 +0000 http://ec2-54-169-116-191.ap-southeast-1.compute.amazonaws.com/?p=2229 Have you ever been to SFS Coffee in Wan Chai, Hong Kong? No? This little coffeeshop is nesteled on the side of the idylic and hip St. Francis Street. Between chic designer shops you can find the perfect spot for a rest.

Once you enter the SFS Coffeeshop you will first find yourself in a little art gallery, displaying the latest creations of local artists, so you can maybe find your next wall decoration while or after having coffee. But SFS offers a lot more: A little garden will make you feel relaxed and if you are having a sweet tooth there are many homemade goodies to satisfy your cravings.

While the barista was grinding the single-origin beans from the UK for my coffee, I had a look at the designer goods displayed. The displayed bags from Ally Capellino made out of canvas and leather are just gorgeous, but I guess they have to wait till Christmas.

Back to my Cappuccino, I get the taste of a great foam and a slightly strong coffee taste, which is just right. Besides Cappuccino you can of course get your Americano, Latte, Espresso and various other drinks as well.
